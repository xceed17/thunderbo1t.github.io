Intro

How Gmaps gives results of place near to your locality, How Ola and other cab apps provide you the nearest cabs available, How short and traffic less paths are found…….It’s not simple data analysis .They are all the real time spatial data. Spatial analysis is a type of geographical analysis which seeks to explain patterns of human behavior and its spatial expression in terms of mathematics and geometry, that is, locational analysis.

Do you think Gmaps gives you all the data you require? Absolutely no. Participants will be taught to download their required map data of any area and to analyze them and to create result layers using open source software. Forgot your old school atlases. How are those maps published now a days? Maps a not just points and marker data they help to visualize simple population data to complex temporal data. We train the participants to publish their own spatial data web mapping.

FAQ

1. Will participation certificate will be provided?
You will receive participation certificate of kurukshetra
2. Do I need to carry laptops?
No. You can bring if possible
3. What will be taught in hands on?
Spatial analysis will be done using simple SQL queries .Location data will be taken from user’s smartphone using some android apps .Web mapping and JS APIs will be taught. Everything will be taught from the basics.
4.Who can participate?
Students from circuit branches (CSE,IT,EEE,ECE), Geography and Civil Engineering can participate. And people who are interested in GIS and map making.
5. What will be the advantage in attending this workshop?
Digital India and many other projects related to government and other large organizations are related to location and spatial data processing. This sector though popular in other countries, is just now developing vigorously in India and is totally a new field. The knowledge from this workshop will make you unique among the other developers.
6. How is it related to GPS location?
As a part of workshop you will be taught to record their GPS paths and to analyze them.
