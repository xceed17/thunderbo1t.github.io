Intro

Wouldn’t it be absolute fun to actually run in Temple Run?! And how exciting it would be if you could create one such application all by yourself? No matter how many ever games we play, virtual games never fail to interest us.Yes! We are coming to teach you the skill of making this magic a reality. Time to learn the two sides of the same coin – augmented reality and virtual reality- together. A workshop where the participants start learning from the basics of AR and VR and move on to advanced concepts all the way till they are equipped with the skills to develop their own apps and experience them using the Google Gears, all in a span of 2 days.This Workshop is not constrained to any departments.
