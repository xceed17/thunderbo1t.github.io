Intro

Do you think you have what is needed to create your own bot? Then, this is the chance. k!urukshetra, India's most celebrated techno-management fest, calls for the Battle of Bots to test your analytical, technical, statistical skills and most importantly, your creativity. Grab this opportunity to solve some real world problems from your perspective, create your own bots and win exciting prices worth 1lakh. So, pull up your socks to run the race of k!nowledge. Bring forth your mind and have a great time!

Format

Round 1

  • It is a preliminary round
  • Basic pen and paper or online test type
  • Teams of 3 (minimum) and 4 (maximum) will be allowed
  • 60-minute duration
  • Starred questions will decide the winner when a tie occurs.

Round 2

  • hands-on round.
  • The teams shall be asked to make a bot with the components provided.
  • The maximum time duration will be 2 hours and 30 minutes.
  • The teams will be evaluated on the basis of Cost of the components used and Time taken.

Finals : Will be informed soon(The winners will be brought to Chennai).
